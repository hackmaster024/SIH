Cosmic
“Cosmic” refers to anything related to the universe or cosmos, including celestial bodies and the vastness of space. It encompasses phenomena like cosmic radiation and cosmic events, highlighting the immense scale and complexity of the universe. The term underscores the vastness and grandeur of the cosmos.
Planetary
"Plan etary" relates to anything associated with planets, including their movements, atmospheres, and physical properties. It covers the study of planets both within our solar system and in distant star systems. This field encompasses various scientific disciplines, such as astronomy, geology, and atmospheric science.
Lunar
“Lunar” refers to anything related to the moon, including its phases, surface features, and phenomena like lunar eclipses. The term comes from the Latin word “luna,” meaning moon. It encompasses the study of the moon’s geology, orbit, and its effects on Earth.
